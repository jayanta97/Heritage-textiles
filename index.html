Why This Archive

India's handloom traditions carry within them centuries of accumulated knowledge — of fibers, dyes, patterns, and ritual. Much of this knowledge exists only in memory and in the hands of aging master weavers. This archive is a race against time: to document, preserve, and transmit what cannot be written in any other way. Cultural continuity depends on our ability to see and record this living science before it is lost.
